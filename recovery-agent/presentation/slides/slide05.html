Core Design Principles

1

Deterministic Safety First

Safety checks are hardcoded, not learned. No LLM in the safety loop.

2

Recovery Decoupled from Workflow

Recovery logic is independent of experiment structure.

3

Explicit Execution Semantics

Contracts, preconditions, postconditions are first-class.

4

Scientific Anomaly =/= Error

Unexpected results may be discoveries, not failures.

Principles derived from FSD and industrial control systems
